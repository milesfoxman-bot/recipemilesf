MILES'S FOOD RECIPE

This is a recipe to make a Sandwitch

INGEDIENTS:

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Avocado
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Carrot
  • Cucumber
  • Water
  • Salt
  • More Meat

Steps:

  1. Get your ingeidents together
  2. Cut bread in half (much as you want to make)
  3. Chop up meat and vegtables to your liking
  4. Put cut bread into toaster
  5. Wait for it to toast - Take out
  6. Put sauce on
  7. Then the meat
  8. Then vegtables
  9. Then anyother toppings you would want
  10. Lastly close it up
  11. And there you have it a Sandwich
